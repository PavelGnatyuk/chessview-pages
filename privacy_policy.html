üîó Third-Party Services

ChessView uses the following external services to provide functionality:

Chess.com Public API

When you enter your Chess.com username, we retrieve your public game data from Chess.com's official API. This data includes:

  • Your public chess games and their details
  • Game moves, timestamps, and results
  • Player ratings (as publicly available on Chess.com)

We do not store your Chess.com credentials or any personal account information.

OpenAI API

When you use the AI analyzer feature, chess positions are sent to OpenAI's API for analysis. This includes:

  • Board position data (piece locations)
  • Game context for accurate analysis

OpenAI processes this data according to their Privacy Policy.
